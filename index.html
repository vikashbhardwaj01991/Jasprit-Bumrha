Who is Jasprit Bumrah?

Born on December 6, 1993, in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, Jasprit Jasbirsingh Bumrah is the heartbeat of India's bowling attack. At 5'10", his hyperextended arm and low-slung release make batsmen duck in fear. Nicknamed the "Yorking King," Bumrah's precision has dismantled lineups in IPL, Tests, ODIs, and T20Is. He plays for Mumbai Indians in IPL and is a vice-captain for India in white-ball cricket.

From a humble background—losing his father young and raised by his resilient mother Daljit—Bumrah turned street cricket into stardom. His 2023 ODI World Cup haul (20 wickets) and IPL dominance (5 titles with MI) define him. Married to Sanjana Ganesan since 2021, with a son Jaisal, he balances family with a vegan, yoga-fueled fitness routine. Injuries like his 2022 back fracture tested him, but comebacks like his 2024 Test hauls prove his grit.

"I don't bowl to impress; I bowl to win." – Jasprit Bumrah
